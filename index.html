MS by Research

Completed my MS-by-Research at IIIT Bangalore under Dr. Neelam Sinha and Dr. G. Srinivasaraghavan. My MS research focused on Complex-valued Neural Networks for MRI reconstruction, with emphasis on efficient k-space interpolation, accelerated MRI pipelines, and improving reconstruction quality in practical clinical settings.
