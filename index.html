I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at IIT Hyderabad under the supervision of Dr. Vineeth Balasubramanian. My Ph.D. research area is Privacy and Safety in AI with a focus on Machine Unlearning.

Previously, I completed my MS-by-Research on Complex-valued Neural Networks for MRI Reconstruction at IIIT-Bangalore with Dr. Neelam Sinha and Dr. G. Srinivasaraghavan, and B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering from VNIT-Nagpur.

Research Interests: Machine Unlearning, Multimodal LLMs, Associative Memory Networks, Certified Unlearning, Domain Adaptation
